# **App Name**: IoT UNKRIS Hub

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Single-Page Navigation: A fluid, single-page scroll interface with 'Scroll-Spy' technology to seamlessly transition between Home, Events, Organization, and About sections.
- Upcoming Events Showcase: An interactive card-based deck featuring upcoming community events with quick-action links and real-time count-down clocks.
- Visual Organization Structure: An interactive hierarchical chart displaying the community's organizational structure with profile modals for each member.
- Masonry Innovation Gallery: A high-performance responsive masonry grid displaying IoT projects, research outputs, and activity snapshots with smooth lightbox previews.
- IoT Research Idea Tool: A feature that acts as a brainstorming tool to generate innovative research prompts combining IoT and AI based on the 'Research and Analysis' slogan.
- Community Inquiry Portal: A professional, validated contact form located at the footer for prospective members and partners to reach out to the university community.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Vibrant Deep Orange (#EA580C) representing energy and the secondary brand color requested.
- Background Color: Off-white (#FAFAFA) to maintain the requested white primary look while ensuring a professional, clean UI depth.
- Accent Color: Dark Sunset Orange (#C2410C) to provide depth for active states, CTAs, and hover effects.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its tech-forward, computerized feel. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for maximum readability and a modern aesthetic.
- Thin-line technical icons related to connectivity, automation, and circuit nodes to mirror the IoT and research theme.
- Modern single-page vertical scroll with generous whitespace to ensure a professional, uncluttered 'business-standard' presentation.
- Hardware-accelerated parallax effects for the hero section and subtle staggered fade-in animations for section components using Framer Motion.
